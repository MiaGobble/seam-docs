v0.5.1 has just a few small changes for quality of life.

<!-- truncate -->

For the most part, errors and warnings have improved tracebacks. In addition, there are more times where a warning or error is thrown intentionally to be descriptive, instead of letting a different error happen.

Some minor adjustments have been made to the codebase, including fixing some shadowed variables.
